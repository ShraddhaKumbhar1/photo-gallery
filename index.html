SK - Visionary 3D Artist

Welcome! I'm SK, a digital sculptor painting with pixels and polygons. My world is one of vibrant textures, impossible architectures, and characters that breathe digital life. Inspired by both the surreal and the hyperreal, I strive to create immersive 3D experiences that challenge perception and evoke emotion.

With a background in traditional sculpting and a passion for cutting-edge technology, I blend techniques to forge unique visual narratives. From stylized character models echoing anime aesthetics to sprawling, atmospheric environments, my work explores the boundaries between fantasy and reality.

This gallery is a curated collection of my personal projects and explorations. Dive in and discover the stories whispered by vertices and the dreams rendered in polygons.
